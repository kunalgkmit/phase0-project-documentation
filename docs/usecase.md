# Use Case Documentation — ExpenseEase

## Primary Use Case
ExpenseEase enables individuals to record, track, and analyze their income and expenses easily.  
It provides a comprehensive financial overview — total balance, categorized charts, and transaction history — all without premium restrictions.

---

## Target Audience
- Individuals seeking a **simple personal finance manager**.  
- Students, freelancers, and professionals wanting full access to expense tracking without payment barriers.  
- Users frustrated by commercial apps that hide critical features behind paywalls.  

---

## App’s USP / Advantages
- **Completely Free:** No in-app purchases or hidden tiers.  
- **Cross-Platform:** One app for both Android, iOS and Web.  
- **Transparent Role System:** Admins can monitor user without invading privacy.  
- **Privacy-First:** Clerk manages authentication securely; no manual password handling.  
- **Real-Time Insights:** Automatic recalculation and live charts.  

---

## Long-Term Vision
- Add budgeting and category tagging for deeper insights.  
- Enable export of reports in PDF/CSV formats.  
- Introduce AI-based spending predictions and financial suggestions.  
- Maintain open access and transparency as the app scales to community use.
